Miscellaneous

  • Comets have two tails: a dust tail (which you can see with the naked eye) and a plasma tail, which is easily photographed but difficult to see with your eyes
  • Depending on the geometry of the Sun, Moon, and Earth, there can be between 2 and 5 solar eclipses each year
  • In the observable universe there are an estimated 2 trillion (2,000,000,000,000) galaxies
  • Together the planets make up 0.14% of the solar systems mass, 99% of which is the gas giants (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune)
  • The combined mass of all the moons in the solar system is less than 0.1% of the Earths mass
